Our Story

A Tradition of Quality and Taste

Minchi Products is a family-owned business located in the historic city of Polonnaruwa, Sri Lanka. Our journey began with a passion for preserving the authentic taste of traditional Sri Lankan snacks, ensuring that the rich culinary heritage continues to be enjoyed by future generations.

We specialize in creating authentic Sri Lankan Murukku and bite products using traditional recipes and methods that have been passed down through generations. Each product we create is made with care, using only the finest locally-sourced ingredients.

At Minchi Products, quality is our priority. Each batch is carefully crafted to ensure the perfect texture, flavor, and crispiness that makes our products stand out in the market.
